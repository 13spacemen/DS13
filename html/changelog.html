29 August 2020

Nanako updated:

  • Yanking out embedded objects will no longer sever the limb.
  • Fixed material weapons having much less health than intended and breaking too easily.
  • You will no longer attempt to block your own attacks, and a weapon can no longer be used to block attacks from itself.
  • Blocking can now only be done with objects of at least normal size. No more fending off slashers with a pill.
  • Normalised rig and backpack storage. RIGs get a bit more, backpacks get a bit less. A standard rig storage now has the same capacity as a backpack
  • Rebalanced plasma saw and laser pick a bit. Both have slightly lower damage, and higher power consumption, but they now also break more slowly.
  • Lots of new items added to the Tools section of the loadout menu!
  • Added the Rivet Gun, a special new tool for patrons. It can be used for longdistance repairs. amd light but consistent damage output.
  • Tool vendors now stock hacksaws, useful for a variety of things, like making barricades!
  • Added two new crafting recipes related to rivet bolts.
  • Bullets and shrapnel now have a chance to ricochet when hitting solid objects.
  • Necromorphs now recieve a 15% reduction to all incoming damage, while standing on corruption. Should help against spawncamping crew assaults.
  • Increased the strength of movespeed effects from walking on corruption. Necromorph movespeed bonus from 20 to 25%, human speed penalty from 35 to 40%
  • Bladed necromorph basic attacks (Slasher, spitter, twitcher, enhanced slasher) now have minor armor penetration, making them better against crew in tough suits.
  • Slasher max health slightly increased, from 75 to 80. Enhanced slasher adjusted proportionately
  • Slasher biomass reclamation time reduced, from 8 minutes to 7, enhanced from 12 to 11
  • Reduced harvester health regen, from 1 to 0.75, should make it easier to kill with sustained assault.

  • Fixed the Kellion Tech's jacket not having an 'open' sprite.

  • Changed Sleep Block A to provide two more beds.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Fixed a bug where grass tiles would appear as nothing.
  • Fixed Hydroponics erroneously showing up as Decommissioned still.
  • Fixed runes not being able to be cleaned by normal means.
  • Aegis asteroid turfs now spawn materials. This also means the mining drill is functional again, and can be used on all turfs. The rewards will be entirely arbitrary.
  • Fixed the ability to disposal lockers.
  • Fixed a rare occurence of spaced players finding their way onto old, deprecated Bay away sites.
  • Removed bags of holding as a valid research creation option.
  • Removed a few xenoarcheology clothing spawns. These were outdated and didn't fit within the universe.
  • Removed a few lunchbox items that were unable to be reworked properly, and were against lore.
  • Added security camera's to Hydroponics.
  • Reworked several lunchboxes to be more lore appropriate.
  • Updated Aegis mines. Ore generation is now completely arbitrary and random within areas specified.
  • Aegis is now surrounded by impassable rocks. This prevents seeing space if you get close enough to the edge.
  • Moderate increase to recharger rates, to prevent the eternal waiting spree of hell.
  • Moderately decreased liver damage sustained from drinking alcohol. This is not a fix for your overindulgence.
  • The ability to drop bombs has now been set to +PERMISSIONS level, which is head devs, head admins and the (vice) host.
  • Significant increase to random ore generation. Aegis 7 should now be more canonical to lore in being mineral rich. Please report issues to Lion.
  • Fixed decals in some instances looking darker than usual. They are now restored.

11 July 2020

Nanako updated:

  • All necromorphs now have partial darkvision, allowing them to see better in dark areas. This can be toggled with 'Toggle Darkvision' in the abilities menu. Not all necromorphs are equal in this regard, leapers and lurkers have better darkvision, ubermorph has the best of all. Exploders get very poor darkvision, as they have their own light source.
  • Slightly buffed the movespeed and regen effects of corruption
  • Fixed pukers being unable to aim and rotate during their vomit attack
  • Fixed a variety of issues where mobs would gain or lose too much speed over time.
  • Reduced the health of all shields by 15%
  • Increased the marker's starting biomass, from 200 to 250, enabling a bit more earlygame power for necromorphs.
  • Increased the minimum delay before evac can be called from 45 minutes to 60 minutes after marker activation.
  • Slightly reduced the melee protection of most kinds of armor, generally by 5 percentage points.
  • Added The Harvester, a powerful new corruption node to control areas, absorb biomass from certain objective objects, and act as a powerful defensive weapon. Comes with three new signal spells to control its attacks.
  • All corruption nodes now regenerate health over time as long as they are on supported corruption
  • All corruption nodes now starve and die over time if they are not on supported corruption
  • Increased the max range and spreading speed of corruption growth nodes (Propagator, Branch, Root)
  • Added the Snare, a new signal ability/corruption node, a stealthy trap for necromorphs to help deter aggression by crew into corrupted areas.

27 June 2020

Nanako updated:

  • Fixed a major calculation bug that was causing necromorph charge attacks to move at roughly half of their intended speed. Some of the speeds have been tweaked to compensate, but overall charging necromorphs move significantly faster now.
  • Fixed charge attacks being broken
  • Reduced windup time and increased flight speed on leaper leap.
  • Charge attacks with autotargeting now pick targets from a 2 tile radius around the clickpoint( previously 1 tile)
  • Redesigned the signal Absorb ability, now with AOE suction, neat visual effects, and can be used around the marker even without corruption!
  • The necromorph spawning menu is now sorted in ascending order of biomass cost.
  • Biomass cost of Exploder significantly increased, from 50 to 65
  • Biomass cost of Leaper slightly reduced, from 85 to 80
  • Lurker biomass cost increased from 50 to 55
  • Lurker windup and cooldown times on spine launch slightly reduced
  • Lurker spine damage slightly increased, from 13 to 14
  • Base biomass reclamation time set to 8 minutes for most necromorphs
  • Slasher biomass cost reduced from 55 to 50. It is now the cheapest of all necromorphs and will remain so.
  • Slasher biomass reclamation time slightly increased from 7.5 mins to 8 mins
  • Twitcher biomass cost slightly reduced, from 130 to 120
  • Removed ancient slasher from spawning menu. It can still spawn as a random variant of a slasher.
  • Humans will now block melee attacks and thrown objects, either with held items or their own arms. Blocking is automatic as long as you face towards the source of the attack, but grab/harm intent will make it more effective. This is a huge increase in human survivability, and several other balance changes have been made to offset it.
  • Blocking attacks now has visual and audio effects.
  • Melee attacks are now guaranteed to hit, both human and necromorph. The new blocking system replaces evasion in melee combat. This change does not affect projectiles or firearms.
  • Slightly increased the damage of many necromorph attacks
  • Slightly reduced the armor values of vintage rig, security rig, and riot armor.
  • Greatly buffed all forms of shields, especially riot shields. However, all held items - including shields - will take damage when used to block attacks, and eventually break.
  • Reduced the number of unitologists that will be picked at roundstart, and during low population times. Increased the maximum limit for high population times.
  • Fixed various issues that occur when a signal or marker player disconnects and reconnects.
  • Significantly increased the damage dealt by thrown objects. Chucking things at necromorphs is a pretty valid combat style now..
  • Added the Heavy Pulse Rifle, a powerful new weapon, currently only obtainable through adminspawning.
