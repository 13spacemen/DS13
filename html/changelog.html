05 April 2020

Nanako updated:

  • The Marker, currently-posessed necromorphs, and signals now each have their own chat style in necrochat, prioritised in that order, so it's easier to tell who's speaking.
  • Admins can now read and mute necrochat
  • Implemented the Puker, a brand new Tier 2 ranged necromorph. Puker is slow, but a flexible fighter and effective at all ranges
  • All necromorph acid attacks now slow the movespeed of their victims while coating the body. This currently applies to spitter, puker, and cysts
  • Slightly reduced necromorph limb health.
  • Added firing audio for cyst corruption nodes.
  • Charging attacks now have a motion blur effect
  • Fixed a variety of animation bugs with leap, step-strike, basic attack animations, and windups for various abilities
  • Charging necromorphs will now fall over if their legs are severed during a charge attack.
  • Sawblades can no longer be worn on the ear
  • Plasma cutter ammo clips now take much less backpack space
  • Randomly generated names are now sane and normal, instead of being a mishmash of syllables
